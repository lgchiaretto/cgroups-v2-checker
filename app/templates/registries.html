Registry Credentials

Configure credentials for private container registries. These are used by skopeo during image inspection.

Credentials are stored locally in the pod

Registry credentials are saved to disk inside the pod. When the pod is restarted or redeployed, credentials in the emptyDir volume are lost.

To persist credentials across restarts, run the following command after adding your credentials:

./setup.sh --persistent

This saves credentials to a Kubernetes Secret and mounts it in the deployment, so they survive pod restarts.

How credentials are used

During a scan, skopeo uses these credentials to authenticate when inspecting images from private registries. Credentials from ImagePullSecrets (extracted from pods) are also used automatically alongside these. The most common registries that require login are quay.io and registry.redhat.io.

Tip: Add your credentials before starting a scan. They only need to last for the duration of the scan.
